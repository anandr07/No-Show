1. Product Overview
1.1 Product Summary

No Show is a multiplayer turn-based card game mobile application where players compete to minimize their card score by forming valid sequences and pairs and strategically declaring "Show" when they believe they have the lowest score.

The application enables:

Real-time multiplayer gameplay

Private and public game rooms

Persistent player scoring across rounds

Player elimination and winner determination

Cross-platform mobile support

1.2 Product Vision

Create a competitive, social, and scalable multiplayer card game platform that allows players to easily play with friends or online players with smooth real-time synchronization.

1.3 Product Objectives
Business Objectives

Acquire users through engaging multiplayer gameplay

Achieve high user retention via competitive scoring

Build scalable multiplayer infrastructure

Enable future monetization (ads, skins, premium features)

Support large concurrent player base

User Objectives

Users should be able to:

Create or join multiplayer rooms

Play with 3–6 players

Play complete matches with multiple rounds

View scores and game progression

Experience fair and synchronized gameplay

Resume game after temporary disconnection

5.4 Game Initialization

System shall:

Step 1: Create 52-card deck

Step 2: Shuffle deck randomly

Step 3: Distribute cards:

Each player receives 7 cards

One random player receives 8 cards

Player with 8 cards starts first

Step 4: Remaining cards form pickup deck

Step 5: Top pickup deck card placed in discard pile

5.5 Turn Flow

Each turn must execute in order:

Step 1: Draw phase

Player must draw 1 card from either:

Pickup deck OR

Discard pile (top card only)

Step 2: Action phase

Player may:

Form sequence

Form pair

Discard one card

Player must end turn with 7 cards.

Step 3: Discard phase

Player discards exactly one card.

Discarded card goes to discard pile.

Step 4: Turn passes clockwise.

5.6 Score Calculation

Score = sum of values of cards remaining in hand.

Example:

Hand:
Ace, 2, 5

Score:
1 + 2 + 5 = 8

5.7 Show Logic

Player may press SHOW during their turn.

System calculates all players' scores.

Let:

Showing player score = S

Other player score = P

Case 1: Any player has score < S

Showing player penalty:

S += 15

Other players:

No change

Case 2: Showing player has lowest score

Each other player:

score += (playerScore − showingPlayerScore)

Showing player:

No change

5.8 Round End

After Show resolution:

System shall:

Update player scores

Start new round

Redistribute cards

Preserve accumulated scores

5.9 Player Elimination

If player score ≥ 99

System shall:

Mark player eliminated

Remove player from future rounds

5.10 Game End

Game ends when only 1 player remains.

That player is winner.
